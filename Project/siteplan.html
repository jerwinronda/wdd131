My Project Site Plan

Site Name

- Jerwin Ronda Professional Hub

Frames my site as a central place for employers and clients to explore my skills and services.

Site Purpose

"My website, Jerwin Ronda Professional Hub, will serve as a central platform to present myself as a professional to both employers and clients. It is designed to showcase my skills, past projects, and achievements in a way that clearly demonstrates the value I can bring to their business or organization. For employers, the site highlights my qualifications, résumé, and case studies that prove my ability to deliver results. For clients, it provides detailed information about the services I offer, testimonials from past collaborations, and a simple way to reach me for proposals or consultations. The overall purpose of this hub is to build trust, establish credibility, and make it easy for decision‑makers to see how I can contribute to their goals."

Scenarios

  • - As an employer, you might ask: “Can I quickly review Jerwin’s résumé and see examples of projects that demonstrate his ability to deliver results?”
  • - As an employer, you might ask: “Where can I find detailed case studies that highlight Jerwin’s problem‑solving skills and technical expertise?”
  • - As a client, you might ask: “What services does Jerwin provide, and how can they add value to my business?”
  • - As a client, you might ask: “Is there a simple way to contact Jerwin to request a proposal or schedule a consultation?”
